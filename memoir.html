The Book

From Exile to Transformation Architect

The complete story — from Kuwait evacuation to Fortune 500 transformation — captured in a memoir coming 2026.

The Narrative

A Story of Crisis, Clarity, and Transformation

Part I: The Exile

1989-1992: Iraq invades Kuwait. A Pakistani family loses everything and returns to a homeland that feels foreign.

"When systems collapse, resilience becomes your first governance framework."

Part II: The Return

1992-2004: Rebuilding in a scarred Kuwait. Cricket becomes the training ground for discipline and strategic thinking.

"Reinvention is not about discarding the past. It is about giving broken things new alignment."

Part III: The Crucible

2004-2016: Fortune 500 transformation at Huawei and Motorola Solutions. Witnessing $100M+ failures and discovering the human layer gap.

"I watched transformations fail not because of technology, but because the human OS underneath couldn't hold."
