Dispatch Issue 02

AI adoption is leadership, not installation.

Most organisations treat AI like a deployment task. In reality, lasting adoption depends on leadership behaviour, integration choices, governance design, and human capability.

The Pyramid

Four layers of real AI adoption

Installation

The easy part, access, setup, tools, pilots.

Integration

Where workflows, roles, and decision paths begin to change.

Governance

Where risk, accountability, and institutional trust are either built or broken.

Capability

The real part, people learning how to think, decide, and operate differently.

The installation trap is assuming the first layer guarantees the rest. It does not. Leadership decides whether AI becomes noise or operating leverage.
